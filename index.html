I'm a Master's student at the Courant Institute (AI concentration), working on the internals of language models — how circuits form, where reasoning breaks, and what interpretability can tell us about alignment.

That background pulls my ML work toward the systems side — writing Triton kernels, thinking about memory hierarchies, and treating training infrastructure as a first-class research artifact rather than a black box.
